SIM Web-UI

The browser as a SIM Scene/Intent surface.

This shell is served by sim-web-shell. It is a thin Scene painter and Intent emitter over the SIM Scene/Intent contracts. Lenses and the Rust web bridge exist in the runtime; the Atelier view opens the generated Site, index, Radar, and Firewall caches.
