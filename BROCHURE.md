# sim-web

In one line: the view and edit surface -- any value shown, edited, and committed on a canvas in the browser.

## What it gives you

Intent capture that turns a click, drag, or typed edit into a checked request before anything changes, a portable scene saved as plain data, a view protocol that always has a sensible default, live canvases for agents, codecs, math, documents, and a full music studio, the pipe that carries your edits out and fresh pictures back wherever the runtime runs, and the program you launch to open the workspace.
